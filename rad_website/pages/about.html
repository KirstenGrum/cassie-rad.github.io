Becoming Rad-ical

Hello, my name is Cassie, and I am Radical! As a former math teacher I have a unique perspective on design and communication. I have spent my design career working with clients from education to retail to develop strategies, concepts, ideas and design with form and function.

I believe that design is a lifestyle and am constantly drawing inspiration from the music I listen to, the clothing I wear, the interiors we live in and art of everyday life. From a menu at a restaurant to a beautiful piece of jewelry you never know where inspriation lives.

Each design challenge comes with a unique solution. When I'm not looking for the next solution I'm probably testing out a new recipe or discussing the finer points of football.
