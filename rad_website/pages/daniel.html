Daniel Ehrenworth

Working with Daniel Ehrenworth is always fun. Both the mailer to promote his "A Series" as well as the poster for his "Naked People" show were all about letting Daniel's work shine. It was about combining his rich images with clean typography and bold bright colors which speak to Daniel's aesthetic.
