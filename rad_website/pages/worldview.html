Worldview Campaign
for Bayview Glen

Bayview Glen was looking for a fresh look to promote the capital campaign for their new build. We developed a clean look ispired by their many worldview's with the circle representing the world but changing color and icon to represent each person's unique view. This color scheme was also used in matching custom photography shot by multiple photographers across the world. The look was carried over multiple platforms and events.
