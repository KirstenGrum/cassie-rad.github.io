Wedding Invitation

Designing a look for a wedding can be difficult, especially when it's your own. For our wedding we wanted something clean and fresh with hints of spring and french elegance. The invitations combined a clean san serif and elegant serif with soft grey and blind letterpressing. This was combined with a custom stamp and handlined envelopes to complete the look.
