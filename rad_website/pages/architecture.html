Architecture And National Identity

To celebrate Canada's Centennial in 1967 each province was given money to design and build a sturcture in it's honour. This book is a look at the structures and Canada's national identity through these pieces of architectural design. The design reflects the simplicity of the desing in that time period as well as the colors and shapes used in the centennial logo.
